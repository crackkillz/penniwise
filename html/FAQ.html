Frequently Asked Questionsc

About Pennywise

What is Pennywise?

Pennywise is a music promotion platform that allows independent artists to share their music and reach a wider audience. It is also a unique listening experience for users and provides valuable data analytics for artists to track their performance.

Why is it called Pennywise?

Pennywise is called that because artists can get a listen for every penny spent on their campaign. This means that their money goes directly towards promoting their music and reaching more listeners.

For Artists

How do I sign up as an artist?

To sign up as an artist, go to the Pennywise website and create an account. From there, you can upload your music and create a campaign by setting a budget and pay-per-play rate.

How much does it cost to upload my music to Pennywise?

It costs a small fee to upload your music to Pennywise. The fee depends on the length of the song and is determined by our pricing algorithm. The fee is competitive and affordable for independent artists.

How much should I set as my pay-per-play rate?

You can set your own pay-per-play rate based on your budget and goals. Keep in mind that the higher the rate, the more unique listeners you will attract, but the faster your campaign balance will be used up.

How do I track my campaign performance?

Pennywise provides data analytics for artists to track their performance. This includes information on the number of plays, unique listeners, and demographics of their audience. This data will help you make more informed decisions about your promotion strategies and provide insight into your target audience.

For Users

How do I make money by listening to music on Pennywise?

Users can make money by listening to music on Pennywise. Each unique listener that plays a song will be paid a small amount from the artist's campaign balance. This encourages users to discover new music and provides artists with valuable exposure.

What makes the listening experience on Pennywise unique?

Instead of shuffling through a playlist, users can listen to a continuous stream of music selected based on their preferences. This creates an engaging experience for users and allows artists to reach a wider audience.

How much does it cost to listen to music on Pennywise?

Listening to music on Pennywise is free for users. You can enjoy a unique listening experience and discover new music while earning money. We hope this FAQ page has answered some of your questions about Pennywise. If you have any further questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us.
